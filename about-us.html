ABOUT HKAF

Have you ever felt a tugging deep within your heart that encouraged you to help make life-changing impacts in the lives of others? His Kingdom Agenda Foundation provides an opportunity to do just that with its mission tagline “Occupy till I come”. As Christians, Ephesians 2:10 declares, our chief aim is to bring glory and honor to God in everything we do.Changing future generations, one child at a time. Matthew 18:1-3 , Raising oaks of righteousness - the planting of The Lord Isaiah 61:3.

HKAF reaches out with the love of Jesus Christ through the means of giving and ministering in parts of the world where there is deep suffering from poverty, spiritual darkness, famine, and other crises. We recognize that when we embody God's heart our priority should be a lifestyle of charity as evidenced by His Word: “This is how we know what love is: Jesus Christ laid down his life for us...

Our Mission

When it comes to raising children in today's world, the old adage “it takes a village” couldn't be more true. At Kings Kids, we recognize that every child is unique; with varied needs, abilities, and potential

Our mission statement is clear: We are committed to helping children reach their fullest potential while showing them all the love they deserve. We strive to provide an environment of acceptance, support, and encouragement that enables our children to become well-rounded and confident life-long learners. By emphasizing social-emotional learning, providing engaging activities and experiences, and partnering with parents - we strive to empower each child to reach their goals.

By teaching our children valuable life skills such as empathy, listening attentively, and problem-solving, we foster a sense of respect for oneself and others - strengthening not just the Kings Kids community but the world at large.

Our Vision

Everyone has a vision in their head of what the ideal world should look like, and we are no exception. Here at HKAF, we have been blessed with the opportunity to make a difference in lives by providing resources and encouragement to children throughout generations.

Our vision statement is to empower this generation to succeed in order to empower the next. We strive to encourage fruit-bearing and sow seeds of excellence into the lives of children so they may reach their full potential. Our mission is focused on changing future generations, one child at a time. We take inspiration from Matthew 18:1-3 and believe that if we plant oaks of righteousness through our guidance, the Lord will make them grow abundantly.

WAYS TO HELP

Supporting HKAF is a meaningful way to contribute to positive social change, and there are various avenues through which individuals can actively participate in these efforts.
 

Donate

One of the most direct ways to support HKAF is through financial contributions. Donations provide essential resources for the implementation of projects, outreach programs, and community initiatives. Even small, regular donations can accumulate into a significant impact, fostering sustained support for the HKAF mission.

 

Participate

Actively participating in the initiatives and programs organized by HKAF is another powerful way to contribute. Volunteering time and skills can make a tangible difference. Whether it involves community outreach, educational workshops, or environmental projects, personal involvement strengthens the impact of the HKAF work and creates a direct connection with the cause.

 

Fundraise

Organizing fundraisers is an effective way to mobilize resources for HKAF. Individuals can initiate their own fundraising campaigns, leveraging social networks, events, or online platforms to raise awareness and gather financial support. This approach not only generates funds but also engages a broader community in the mission of the HKAF.

 

Contribute

Contributions to HKAF extend beyond monetary donations. Individuals can contribute expertise, knowledge, and skills that align with the organization's goals. Professionals, such as educators, healthcare workers, or IT specialists, can offer their services pro bono to enhance the impact of specific projects and programs.
